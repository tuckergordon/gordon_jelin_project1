James Jelin and Tucker Gordon

Bowdoin College, 3 November 2016

How To Use:

First, you select a sex by clicking male or female. Then you pick an age by clicking 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, or 22. Then, you select a family size (more or less than 3), and finally you select 'does extracurriculars' or 'does not do extracurriculars'. As you click one of these, the options for the next one are revealed. At the end, text will appear that lists the avg. weekend alcohol consumption for students in that particular grouping. You can select other attributes, too, in order to see more groupings, and deselect attributes at any time.
